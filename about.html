About One Tribe Coffee & Restaurant

A modern coffee house and restaurant where great food, rich Ugandan coffee, and community come together.

Our Story

One Tribe Coffee & Restaurant was created to offer authentic Ugandan coffee alongside freshly prepared fast foods in a welcoming environment.

  • Espresso drinks and traditional beverages
  • Fresh fast foods and full meals

  • Friendly and welcoming service
